Our Services

Select Your Top Candidates

With Emplow, it just takes one glance to know whether a candidate meets your criteria or not. See a holistic shortlist of your top candidates who match both job and personality requirements.

Know Your Candidate

The job market is 90% candidatedriven. Thus, it is more crucial than ever to understand and relate to your candidate. Emplow shows you, how you can build up a fantastic relationship with your candidate using personality insights.

Save time because time is money

Long hiring processes are one of the biggest frustrations and inefficiencies. Through automatic CV scraping and personality assessment, Emplow helps you to significantly reduce your time spent on screening, assessing, and onboarding applicants.

Powered by AI

Emplow is a software that uses AI to understand a candidate’s personality without any surveys involved. This allows for a fast and bias-free assessment.
